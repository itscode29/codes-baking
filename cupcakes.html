Nutella Cupcakes

Ingredients

For the cupcakes
  • 150g unsalted butter
  • 130g granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 200g all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 100ml milk
  • 4 tablespoons (or more) Nutella
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
For the buttercream
  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 150g powdered sugar
  • 2 teaspoons milk
  • 4 tablespoons Nutella

Preparation

Cupcakes

Beat the softened butter with the sugar. Then add the vanilla and, one by one, the egg yolks, leaving the egg whites separate, as you have to beat them until stiff and add them later. Now add the flour, together with the baking powder and the cinnamon, sifting it all. Now add the milk and then the egg whites beaten until stiff. Remember to add the egg whites little by little, folding them in. Finally add the Nutella. Now spread the batter in the capsules and bake for approximately 20-23 minutes at 180ÂºC.
